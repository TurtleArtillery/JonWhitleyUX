About Us

Hi! So by now you’ve seen my work and you might be wondering, “gosh, who is this person who has designed such wonderful user-experiences?”

The short answer is: I’m Jon.

A slightly longer answer is: I’m an Interaction Designer. My specialties are UX Design and Information Architecture, with a focus in mobile design.

I graduated Magna-Cum-Laude from UT Dallas in 2014 with a BA in Arts and Technology and a Minor in Arts and Performance. I also enjoy Writing, Animation, Video/Photography, Graphic Design, Programming, and Game Design.

But who am I as a person?

I could write a whole long entry about my personality, but that’s probably too heavy, so let’s just do this FAQ style:



Q: What tools do I work with?

A: Sketch, Axure RP, Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Q: Can I write code?

A: Yeah, some. I can do light scripting. I like to use it to drive animation.

Q: Am I a developer?

A: No. I’m a designer who can code.

Q: Can I translate between designers and engineers?

A: Absolutely.

Q: Am I for or against the Oxford Comma?

A: For, definitely for.

Q: Am I an introvert or an extrovert?

A: Introverted, generally speaking.

Q: Dogs or cats?

A: Dogs. Cats can be good, but I prefer dogs.

Q: What do I do for fun?

A: Write fiction, study languages, make cookies.

Q: Are my cookies any good?

A: You bet.

Q: What languages do I speak?

A: English natively, and my German and Spanish aren’t so bad. I also studied some Latin.

Q: Am I funny?

A: Is anyone who says they’re funny ever actually funny? That being said, I love puns. Like this, “Why can’t seagulls fly over bays? Because then they’d be bagels!” (Get it? Bay-Gulls?)

Q: Have I written anything good?

A: I like to think so. I wrote a bunch of screenplays, some Game story-scripts, and a novel.

Q: Anything published?

A: Nope. I just like writing.
